About Me

"The Maltese is a toy dog covered from head
to foot with a mantle of long, silky, white hair.
They are gentle-mannered and affectionate,
eager and sprightly in action, and, despite
their size, possessed of the vigor needed for
the satisfactory companion. Size: Weight under
7 pounds, with from 4 to 6 pounds preferred.
Overall quality is to be favored over size."
